Company History

Tsirin Motors Limited (TML) is a family-owned SME founded in 1992 by Joseph Sukaliana. Originally established as Tsirin Mechanical Repair, the business focused on vehicle repairs, spare parts sales, refrigeration, and air conditioning services with a team of 12. In 1994, TML was incorporated under the Investment Promotion Authority Act and became Tsirin Motors Ltd.

Between 1994 and 1997, TML acquired land in Buka Town and built a two-story building for operations and office tenants. Today, the company employs 21 staff and offers diverse services including office space rentals, motor vehicle spare parts sales, and authorized PNGAIR services for ticketing and ground handling.

In 2014, TML secured a major contract with PNGAIR for ticketing services, followed by a ground handling contract in 2017. TML continues to expand its services to meet the growing needs of Bougainville and Papua New Guinea.
